Track Yourself

Track Yourself is a web app that allows you to keep track of various behaviors like exercise, sleep and water intake to see how these behaviors affect your overall well-being. Enter data on the forms page to record how your behaviors and view the dashboard to see graphical representations of your behavior patterns. Sign up to track yourself.

Barry Snoozer

I'm always complaining to my friends about how tired I am. Violet suggested I try using Track Yourself to track my sleep. After entering in my sleep times for a few weeks, I was surprised to learn I was only sleeping an average of 3 hours a night. I'm going to keep recording my sleep times with a goal of getting a least 6 hours a night.
